# Product Truth — AI Thinking Partner

## Problem

I currently split my mental tracking across Excel (habits), Obsidian (journaling),
and scattered notes for thoughts that arise from emotions. These tools store
information, but they do not work together to help me see repetition, interrupt
unproductive thinking loops, or ask the one question that could shift my thinking
in the moment. As a result, I over-document, over-analyze, and still feel mentally
stuck or uncertain about whether a day was actually “used well”.

## Promise

This product consolidates my habit tracking, structured journaling, and free-form
thoughts into one place, asks one well-timed question when it helps, shows patterns
without judgment or interpretation, and stays quiet when speaking would make my
thinking worse.

## Anti-Promise

This product will never:
- Act as a therapist or attempt to solve my problems
- Try to motivate, reassure, or comfort me
- Give unsolicited advice
- Interpret my habits, emotions, or thoughts for me
- Optimize me for productivity or output
- Generate long explanations or analysis
- Replace my judgment with its own conclusions

When it shows patterns, it will only surface repetition and frequency, not causes,
meanings, or recommendations.

## Primary User

The primary user is me: someone who already reflects daily, tracks habits carefully,
overthinks decisions, values precision over comfort, and wants tools that reduce
cognitive noise rather than generate insight for its own sake.
