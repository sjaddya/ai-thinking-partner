# Journal Template v1

These prompts are static and not AI-generated.
They belong to the Structured Journal Layer.

## Morning Intention Prompts (Static)
1. What single outcome must be true by end of day?
2. What is the first focused work block today?
3. What constraint is most likely to disrupt today?
4. What is the minimum acceptable day if plans change?
5. What evidence will show the day was intentional?

## Evening Reflection Prompts (Static)
1. What actions were completed today as facts?
2. What repeated pattern appeared today?
3. Where were facts and interpretation mixed?
4. What thought loop appeared most often?
5. What remains open without a next step tonight?

## Emotion Logging Prompts (Static)
1. Which emotion label best fits right now?
2. What is the intensity from 0 to 10?
3. Where is it felt most clearly in the body?
4. Is the intensity rising, stable, or falling?
5. What thought is present alongside this emotion?

## Separation Rules
- Emotion logging prompts are separate from Thinking Partner questions.
- Logging an emotion is treated as Emotional Processing mode.
- After emotion logging, allow exactly one follow-up question from the thinking partner question bank.
- No cause analysis, no resolution attempts, and no reassurance.
